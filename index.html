My Projects

Photography

Photography is definitely my first, and the greatest passion. I was a kid (i think i was maybe 12 y.o) when i discovered it in myself, that taking pictures makes me feel genuinely happy and satisfied. It took me about 6 years to buy my first entry-level digital single-lens camera - Canon EOS 500D. Before that i was using... digital compact canon PowerShot. And i have to say: good equipement helps to achive your aims in less painfull and definitely faster way but it will not do good picture by itself. This, lasting for already 20 years, adventure taught me a lot about where do i actually want to be in photography world. And how would i like to connect it with my other passions. Currently i am a proud owner of: Canon EOS D6.
